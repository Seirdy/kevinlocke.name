Common Spice Mixes

Barbecue Rub

Sweet and smoky with a little heat.

PartsIngredient
4Brown sugar
2Paprika
2Garlic
2Onion
1Salt
1Hickory smoke
Cayenne pepper
Rosemary
Mustard
Lemon zest

Rub onto meats before cooking.

Bouquet Garni

A French bundle of herbs tied together for use in soups, stocks, and stews.

PartsIngredient
2Parsley
1Thyme
1Bay leaf
Basil
Rosemary
Savory
Tarragon

Bind or wrap herbs with twine or cheesecloth. Submerge in dish during cooking, remove before serving.

2Coriander
2Cumin

1Red chili flakes

Ginger

Fines Herbes feen-ZAIRB

Classic French seasoning with bright/fresh flavor.

PartsIngredient
1Parsley
1Chives
1Chervil
1Tarragon

Warm spice common in South Asian cuisine.

PartsIngredient

6Coriander
4Cardamom

2Cloves
1Mace (or nutmeg)
